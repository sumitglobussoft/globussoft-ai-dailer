FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Does the AI really sound human in Hindi?
Yes. We use state-of-the-art TTS providers like Sarvam AI and ElevenLabs that produce natural, conversational Hindi and Hinglish speech. Most leads cannot distinguish our AI from a real human caller. The voice handles interruptions, pauses, and colloquial phrases naturally.
How does pricing work? Are there hidden fees?
Pricing is simple per-minute billing. You purchase a plan with a set number of minutes, and you're charged only for connected call time. There are no setup fees, no per-seat charges, and no lock-in contracts. Unused minutes roll over for 30 days.
Can the AI handle objections and questions from leads?
Absolutely. The AI is trained on your product knowledge base and common objection-handling scripts. It can answer product questions, handle pricing objections, address timing concerns, and smoothly guide conversations toward booking an appointment. You can customize the AI's persona and responses.
Which CRMs do you integrate with?
We integrate with HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho CRM, and Freshsales out of the box. For other CRMs, we offer webhook-based integrations and a REST API. Our Enterprise plan includes custom CRM integration with dedicated engineering support.
How quickly can I get started?
You can launch your first campaign within 30 minutes. Just sign up, upload a CSV of leads, configure your AI agent's script and persona, and hit dial. No technical setup, no coding, no lengthy onboarding. Our team is available to help with initial configuration.
